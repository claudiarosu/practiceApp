# Bucharest Education System Context

## Overview
Bucharest is divided into 6 administrative sectors. Each sector has its own local administration, but the education system is unified under the Ministry of Education and the Bucharest School Inspectorate (ISMB).

## School Types
- **Grădiniță (Kindergarten):** Ages 3-6. Can be public or private.
- **Școală Gimnazială (Primary & Middle School):** Grades 0-8. Ending with "Evaluarea Națională" exam.
- **Liceu (High School):** Grades 9-12. Entry is based on the admission grade (media de admitere).
- **Școală Internațională:** Private schools following international curricula (Cambridge, IB, etc.).

## Evaluation & Metrics
- **Evaluarea Națională:** The national exam at the end of 8th grade. Scores are a key metric for parents.
- **Bacalaureat:** The final exam at the end of 12th grade.
- **Grading:** 1-10 scale, where 10 is the highest.
- **ARACIP:** The agency responsible for quality assurance in pre-university education.

## Geographic Organization
- **Sectors 1-6:** Parents often filter by sector or proximity to subway stations (Metrorex).
- **Proximity (Vicinage):** For public primary schools, priority is given to children living in the school's assigned district (circumscripție).
